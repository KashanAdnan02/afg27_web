Description:

In many remote villages of Kandahar, families must walk hours to reach basic healthcare. This
mobile clinic ensures medical care arrives directly at their doorstep.

Each week, ARCS teams travel to underserved communities to:

Vaccinate children against preventable diseases.
Provide maternal and newborn care.
Distribute essential medicines and supplies.
Train local volunteers in first aid and hygiene practices.

Story of Hope

“For the first time, my children received vaccinations without walking for hours. The clinic comes to us, and it saves lives.”

Impact Highlights

300+ children vaccinated in the first month.
60 pregnant women received safe maternal check-ups.
